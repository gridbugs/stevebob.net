what is this?

Heroes and Villains is a game played by a group of people in a room. At the beginning of the game, each person chooses someone to be their 'hero' and someone else to be their 'villain'. The aim of the game is for each person to position themselves in the room such that their hero is between them and their villain. This is a computer simulation of heroes and villains with dots representing people. (hide info)

Hero and Villain Assignment

Basic:
One Common Hero
One Common Villain

Advanced:
d is a dot
h(d) denotes the hero of d
v(d) denotes the villain of d
By default these functions are not injective.
h(v(d)) = d for most d
v(h(d)) = d for most d
h(h(d)) = d for most d
v(v(d)) = d for most d
make h injective
make v injective

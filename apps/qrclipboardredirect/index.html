QR Clipboard

Copy from your browser. Paste to your phone.

Sometimes there is some text that appears on your computer, such as a phone number or URL that you want to enter on your phone. So what do you do? Manually type it in like a caveman ()? Well Mr. Caveman (), those days are over.

Downloads

Chrome Extension

Install Via Chrome Web Store

What is this?

This is an Android app and Chrome extension for simplifying transferring data from computers to phones. Text that has been copied to the computer's clipboard is encoded in a QR code by the Chrome extension. The Android app then reads the QR code, decodes it, and stores the text in the phone's clipboard. The text can be pasted, as if it was copied on the phone itself.

Here is a screenshot.

To copy text from you computer to your phone:

  1. Copy some text.
  2. Click the QR Clipboard icon in your browser (). A QR code will appear.
  3. Launch the QR Clipboard Android app, and point the camera at the QR code on your computer screen.
  4. Wait for the phone's screen to flash white.
  5. The clipboard on your phone now contains the copied text.
